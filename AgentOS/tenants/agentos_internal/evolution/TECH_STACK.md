## Operational Constraints

- **Zero external calls** — all services are Zo-local
- **Headless** — no GUI required; all management via terminal
- **Stateless execution** — kernel reads/writes TCOs only; no in-memory global state
- **Idempotent bootstrap** — `bootstrap.sh` is safe to re-run at any time
